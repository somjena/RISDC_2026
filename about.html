RISDC 2026

About The Conference

International Conference on Recent Innovations in Sustainable and Digital Construction

Conference Overview

The International Conference on Recent Innovations in Sustainable and Digital Construction (RISDC-2026) brings together leading researchers, industry professionals, policymakers, and academicians to explore groundbreaking advancements shaping the future of construction.

Our Mission

To foster collaboration and knowledge exchange that accelerates the adoption of sustainable practices and digital technologies in the construction industry worldwide.

500
Expected Participants
50
International Speakers
3

Conference Themes

Sustainable Construction

  • Green building materials
  • Energy-efficient design
  • Circular economy in construction
  • Life cycle assessment

Digital Transformation

  • Building Information Modeling (BIM)
  • Digital twins in construction
  • AI and machine learning applications
  • IoT and smart construction sites

Innovation & Technology

  • Robotics and automation
  • 3D printing in construction
  • Advanced construction materials
  • Smart infrastructure systems

Conference Objectives

01

Knowledge Exchange

Facilitate the exchange of cutting-edge research, innovative ideas, and practical experiences among academicians, researchers, and industry professionals.

02

Industry-Academia Collaboration

Bridge the gap between academic research and industry practice by fostering collaborations that address real-world construction challenges.

03

Sustainable Development

Promote sustainable construction practices that minimize environmental impact while maximizing economic and social benefits.

04

Digital Transformation

Accelerate the adoption of digital technologies in construction to improve efficiency, safety, and project outcomes.
